Who am I

I am a strong, independent woman who is not afraid to speak her mind and a high-spirited magician whose speciality is gossip, competition, and drama. Although at first sight, I may seem superficial, I am a keen observer. I'm incredibly smart but have trouble with diplomacy. I know I am mentally stronger than any man, as I have shown to be the most rational of the group during stressful situations, often being mistaken for cruel and bitter. Once I allow someone into my life, I can be kind, caring and will stop at nothing for my friends and for Fillory.
